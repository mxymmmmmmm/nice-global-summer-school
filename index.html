From 29 July to 11 August 2026, the 1st NICE Global Summer School will take place at Tongji University. Co-organised by the Tongji Sino-Finnish Centre and the College of Design and Innovation, the summer school is themed "China–Northern Europe Co-Creation: A Living Lab for Future Human Flourishing." The programme will bring 20 outstanding students from leading European innovation universities to Tongji. Together with these European students, you will learn, conduct field research, design, and create in cross-cultural teams.
2026年7月29日至8月11日，第一届 NICE Global Summer School 将在同济大学举行。本次夏校由同济大学中芬中心与设计创意学院联合主办，以"中国—北欧共创：人类未来幸福生活现场"为主题，邀请20位来自欧洲顶尖创新大学的优秀学生来到同济，与同济学生共同学习、调研、设计与创造。
The summer school will be guided by an international teaching team of professors from Delft University of Technology, Aalto University, the University of Lapland, and Tongji University, with a focus on cutting-edge areas including wellbeing design, interaction design, service design, and social innovation.
本次夏校导师团队由来自荷兰代尔夫特理工大学、芬兰阿尔托大学、芬兰拉普兰大学及同济大学的教授组成，聚焦福祉设计、交互设计、服务设计与社会创新等前沿方向。
This is a high-intensity, fully English, cross-cultural co-creation summer school. Selected Tongji students will work in mixed teams with European students on wellbeing innovation challenges. Through real-world community, urban, cultural, and enterprise settings, teams will complete a full design process: field research, problem framing, concept development, prototyping, and final presentation.
这是一次高强度、全英文、跨文化的国际共创夏校。入选同济学生将与欧洲学生组成混合团队，围绕福祉创新相关议题，在真实社区、城市、文化与企业场景中完成调研、问题定义、概念生成、原型制作和最终展示。
We welcome Tongji students with an open mindset, the ability to collaborate in English, cross-cultural awareness, and strong creative potential. The programme is open to current Tongji master's and doctoral students from all academic disciplines and backgrounds. Whether you study design, engineering, architecture, computer science, business or any other field, you are encouraged to apply if you are interested in future wellbeing, innovation, and cross-cultural collaboration. The programme lasts two weeks, and selected students must participate fully in all lectures, fieldwork, company visits, workshops, teamwork, prototyping, and final presentations. Please apply only after confirming that you have the time and energy to fully commit. Once admitted, students are expected to honour their commitment and should not withdraw without a valid reason or attend selectively.
我们期待具有开放视野、全英文协作能力、跨文化沟通意识和创新能力的同济学生报名。本项目面向同济大学所有专业背景的硕士生和博士生开放。无论你来自设计、工程、建筑、计算机、管理、或其他学科，只要你对未来福祉、创新实践和跨文化合作充满兴趣，都欢迎申请。项目为期两周，入选同学须全程参加，完整投入课程学习、实地调研、企业参访、工作坊共创、原型制作和成果展示等全部环节。请在确认时间与精力允许后再提交申请；一经录取，应认真履行参与承诺，不得无故中途退出或选择性参加。
